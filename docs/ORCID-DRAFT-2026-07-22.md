### battery-foundation-encoder — HELD, not published (leakage found)
Working title: *"Does Cheap Adaptation Make a Foundation Model Worth It? LoRA vs From-Scratch for
Early-Cycle Battery Life."* A side-paper comparing a LoRA-adapted MOMENT-1 time-series foundation
model against a small from-scratch transformer for early battery RUL prediction.
**Status: HELD, as of 2026-07-21/22 — do NOT list as complete or post to arXiv.** The repo's own
`README.md` still says "EXPERIMENT COMPLETE — result frozen," but that certification was issued
2026-06-08 and has since been **overtaken by a confirmed data-leakage finding**: per
`Pantheon/karnameh-2026-07-21.html` and `battery-foundation-encoder/docs/PENDING-AFTER-RERUN-2026-07-22.md`,
label leakage was confirmed in the input-length range L=200–500 (shared with the thesis pipeline),
which contaminates the previously-reported headline numbers in that range. The L≤100 results are
described as clean, but the paper as a whole is on hold pending a per-bin ISSE re-run. **Not posted
to arXiv. Do not cite specific numbers from this paper as final.** Source:
`battery-foundation-encoder/README.md`, `docs/PENDING-AFTER-RERUN-2026-07-22.md`,
`docs/reviews/LEAKAGE-INVESTIGATION-2026-07-21.md`, `Papers/PAPERS.md`.

### diffbatt-plus — in progress, unpublished
Improving DiffBatt (Eivazi, Hebenbrock et al., NeurIPS 2024) — a diffusion model for battery RUL,
from the supervisor's group. **Private/local, in progress; not submitted or published anywhere.**
Baseline reproduced on the ISSE box (MIX RUL RMSE 207.5, vs. paper's 202±6); a Tier-0
inference-only improvement found (207.5 → 194.4, −6.3%, no retraining, fair ref-free estimator);
Tier-1+ retraining experiments (min-SNR, inter-cell conditioning) scoped/scaffolded but largely
unrun as of the last update. Source: `diffbatt-plus/README.md`, `RESULTS.md`.

### fbg-multimodal-early-rul — idea/method/code done, no results yet
*"FBG×E — Multimodal Early-Cycle RUL"* — asks whether early-cycle optical (Fiber Bragg Grating)
signals add predictive value for battery remaining life beyond electrical signals alone.
**Status: framework, experiment design, and code are complete; zero experimental results exist.**
Gated on partner (FiSens / CircularLIB) data access and IP sign-off. Local-only; publishing is
explicitly on hold pending partner approval. **Do not list as a work with results — at most, list as
"in preparation."** Source: `fbg-multimodal-early-rul/README.md`.

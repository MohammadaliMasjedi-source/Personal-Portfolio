## What to send next

1. **A teaching / lesson shot — required.** You with a student, or leading a class: you
   pointing at a fretboard, correcting a hand position, or a student playing while you
   listen/instruct. *Why it matters:* your own bio leads with "teacher" — 18+ years,
   Forough Music Academy, the Fars Music Policy Council — and the gallery currently shows

2. **One shot from a different occasion or venue** — anything NOT from the Nov 2023
   Clausthal-Zellerfeld night: an older concert, a rehearsal, a recital back in Iran, a
   different German venue. *Why it matters:* signals "years of this," not "one gig that got
   photographed a lot."
3. **A candid, off-stage moment** — tuning up, the case open before a show, talking with
   another musician, warming up backstage. Unposed. *Why it matters:* every current photo
   is mid-performance/on-stage; one candid breaks the "performance reel" feel and matches
   the honest, unpolished tone the rest of the site is going for.
4. **A composing/poetry angle (nice-to-have).** You with sheet music, a notebook, or a
   Khayyám collection next to the guitar. *Why it matters:* your own copy says "much of what
   I play is bound to Persian poetry" — nothing on the page currently shows that side of it.

**Target:** #1 is the minimum — send at least that one. Ideally all four: that takes the
gallery from 5 photos to 9 (1 feature + 8 grid = two clean rows of 4 on desktop, four rows
of 2 on mobile). If you only have two or three, send those — it's still a real improvement,
and the grid can be adjusted to fit cleanly at any count. Don't hold off just to hit a round
number.

## Practical notes

- **No watermarks or logos baked into the photo.** That's exactly why
  `assets/img/worlds/music-hall.jpg` got cut today (burned-in "MoMas" mark, kept on disk,
  not shown). If a photo has a studio or venue logo stamped on it, send the clean version if
  you have one.
- **If a student is recognizable in the teaching shot,** get their (or their parent's, if a
  minor) OK before it goes on a public site — or pick a framing where they're not
  identifiable (over-the-shoulder, back to camera, hands-and-fretboard only).
- **Orientation and quality:** landscape or portrait both work — the grid crops to fit.
  Just send the original, uncropped, at whatever resolution your phone or camera gives you.
- **Where they go:** drop files in `assets/img/gallery/`, named like the existing ones
  (e.g. `teach-lesson-01.jpg`, `perform-[venue]-01.jpg`, `candid-backstage-01.jpg`) — or
  just send them to me directly with one true sentence about each (roughly where/when) and
  I'll wire them into the gallery with an honest caption, same as the other five.

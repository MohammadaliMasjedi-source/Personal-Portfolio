- So: person-name carries reputation; brand-name carries products + funding + succession.

## The design bar — a LEVEL to reach, not styles to copy (Mo's note, 2026-07-19)
Mo shared these to show **how far our current designs are from real craft** — not as templates to stamp on things.
Take the *quality*, give each project its own fitting look.
- `cenee-paris.com`, `fieldstudiesflora.com` — the reachable craft level: restraint, real typographic hierarchy,
  generous whitespace, excellent imagery, one strong idea.
- `bruno-simon.com`, `rleonardi.com` — the dream tier (magnum-opus, months of specialist work). Borrow the
  *spirit* (one signature delight moment + humor when suited), don't clone.
- `awwwards.com` — keep pulling references.
**What "primitive" means (avoid):** crowded layouts, default fonts, weak hierarchy, stock feel, decoration
instead of one clear idea. **The rule:** every visual thing gets its own design, built to *this level*, run past
the design-critic and iterated — Mo never sees a primitive first draft.

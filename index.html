— About

I refuse to fit in one box.

Mo
— Mo

I'm Mohammadali “Mo” Masjedi — from Iran, now in the Harz mountains of Germany.

By day I build machine-learning models that read the future of batteries. But a person is more than a job title. I paint and create, I train and compete, I lead and organize, and I write poetry when the equations go quiet.

The thread through all of it is the same: curiosity, craft, and the stubborn belief that the interesting things happen where disciplines collide. This site is a map of those collisions.

  • RootsIran — engineering, art, and a love of words.
  • 2024–2025Graduate research at TU Clausthal, Germany.

The Manager

Scattered effort into shared momentum.

Leading is a craft of its own — clarity, trust, and turning a group of people into a team with a direction. A few principles I lead by:

01

Clarity first

People move fast when they know exactly where “done” is.

02

Trust by default

Hire smart people, give them room, remove their blockers.

03

Decide, then learn

A good decision today beats a perfect one too late.

04

Credit out, blame in

Wins belong to the team; the misses are on the lead.
